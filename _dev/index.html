a front end infrastructure

overview

introduction

BoxTent is a front end workflow powered by Grunt and Bower that gives you the infrastructure to kickstart your next HTML+CSS+JS project. It is particularly efficient on small websites (like this one), but can be easily extended with extra features to suit your needs.

BoxTent comes packed with tasks automation and optimizations to help you stay focused on what really matters: your code.

BoxTent covers all basic needs of a modern day front-end web developer:

  • browser auto-reloading
  • Browser syncing across devices
  • images optimization
  • .scss compilation with Compass support
  • .js concatenation
  • .html, .css and .js minification
  • .css autoprefixing
  • critical above the fold .css management
